Notable African American Figures

Learn about some influential leaders who shaped history through courage, activism, culture, and creativity.

Harriet Tubman

Harriet Tubman helped lead enslaved people to freedom through the Underground Railroad and showed incredible courage. She became a symbol of strength and hope for African Americans.

Frederick Douglass

Frederick Douglass escaped slavery and became a powerful speaker and writer. He used his voice to fight for freedom, equal rights, and education for African Americans.

Malcolm X

Malcolm X was a strong civil rights leader who encouraged Black pride and self-empowerment. He inspired people to stand up against racism and fight for justice.

Maya Angelou

Maya Angelou was a poet and author whose writing shared the struggles and strength of African Americans. Her stories inspired people to overcome challenges and believe in themselves.
